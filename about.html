ABOUT US

Technology is the cornerstone of modern business success. We help organizations harness the power of technology to drive innovation, improve efficiency, and achieve strategic objectives through comprehensive consulting services and enterprise-grade solutions.

Vertical Consulting LLC was founded by professionals with extensive hands-on experience in enterprise technology management. Our expertise includes managing large-scale cloud infrastructure portfolios, leading critical system migrations, and ensuring security compliance in highly regulated industries.

We have successfully delivered technology solutions for healthcare organizations, managing 120+ virtual servers across AWS, Azure, and private cloud environments. Our experience includes leading strategic migrations of enterprise tools like Atlassian Suite, implementing HIPAA-compliant security frameworks, and optimizing infrastructure for scalability and performance.

Our leadership extends beyond technical expertise, with experience in cross-cultural team management, non-profit operations, and servant leadership that brings a unique perspective to client relationships and project delivery.
