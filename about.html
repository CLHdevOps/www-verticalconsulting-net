ABOUT US

We are passionate about everything digital and are trying to make a name for ourselves in the web development and UX industries.

Trained as a psychologist with a programming background, Brandon Dreslin gained an interest in creating unique and functional digital products. He understands how human factors impacts design, and wants to help others realize their dreams. Thus, Dreslin Consulting Group was formed.

Brandon is a junior at Embry-Riddle Aeronautical University majoring in human factors psychology. Additionally, he is pursuing a computer programming specialist certificate with Java specialization at St. Petersburg College.

His education and diverse skill set — combined with a drive to provide a quality product — are Brandon's greatest strengths.
